The Cardigans - Welcome

The Cardigans’ sound is often described as an eclectic fusion of pop, rock, and alternative influences. Alongside catchy melodies, thoughtful lyrics, and lead vocalist Nina Persson’s distinctive voice, their playful approach has resulted in beloved hits like “Lovefool,” “My Favourite Game”, “Step on Me” and over 15 million sold albums – globally. Besides making their mark on the charts, their music has been used to enhance cult TV-series and movies like Baz Luhrmann’s Romeo & Juliet, The Office and The Crown. Further enriching their legacy and solidifying their place in ’90s pop culture and beyond.

The Rest Of The Best Vol. 1 & 2 Out Now

Decades after capturing hearts worldwide with hits like 'Lovefool' and 'My Favourite Game,' The Cardigans return with 'The Rest Of The Best Vol. 1 & 2'. These releases compile rare recordings, alternate takes, international-only releases, and beloved B-sides that showcase the band's versatility beyond their mainstream success. If you're rediscovering the band's playful 'Life' era B-sides or encountering their sophisticated 'Long Gone Before Daylight' outtakes for the first time, this collection will reaffirm why The Cardigans remain one of pop music's most enduring and creatively rich acts.
